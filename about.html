Amidst the hustle and bustle of the silicon valley of India, If you feel that nothing is going right, you should perhaps head to Ramya Resort Bangalore - an oasis of absolute peace and tranquillity, tucked in lush greenery. The tall trees swaying in the gentle breeze, as if singing a welcome song for you; the chirping of the birds hiding in shrubs and behind the trees, will welcome you on your arrival at the resort.

If you are wondering how your day could be different from that of a mundane day, well we tell you how it exactly feels to be here. As the sun wakes up from a deep slumber, freshen up and try your hands at volley ball, badminton, basketball, football and a host of other outdoor and indoor games. Radiant Resort Bangalore also has a separate kids play area where children can have fun to the utmost.
